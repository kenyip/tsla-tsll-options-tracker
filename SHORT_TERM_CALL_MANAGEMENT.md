# Short-Term Call Management Rules (v6.6)

## How to Manage the 435 Call if TSLA Shoots Up (e.g. to 453)

**Current Position**: Sell 435 Call, expires **May 16, 2026 (Friday)**, 5 DTE

### Management Plan if Price Moves Against Us

| Scenario | Action | Why |
|----------|--------|-----|
| **Price > 440** (early warning) | Buy to close 50% of position | Lock in small loss, reduce risk |
| **Price > 450** (like 453) | **Roll up & out** to 455 Call, May 23 | Collect more credit + more time |
| **Price > 460** | Close the entire position | Accept the loss, switch back to puts |

### Key Rules
- Max loss = 2x credit received (do not let it go beyond this)
- Always have a plan before entering the trade
- Short-term calls require daily monitoring (unlike 30 DTE trades)

**Recommendation**: If it goes to 453, roll the 435 Call to the 455 Call for May 23. This gives you more room and usually collects additional credit.
